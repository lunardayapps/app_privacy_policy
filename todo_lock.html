Todo Lock — Private Photo & Video Vault

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Todo Lock is designed with privacy as its core principle. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. This includes:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No photos or videos
  • No usage data or analytics
  • No crash reports sent to any server
  • No device identifiers
  • No location data

Data Storage

All content stored in the app — including photos, videos, tasks, and your vault password — is saved exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Third-Party Services

Todo Lock does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services. There are no third parties that receive any data from this app.

Internet Access

Todo Lock does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

Account Registration

Todo Lock does not require or support user accounts of any kind. No sign-up, login, or email address is needed.

Children's Privacy

Todo Lock does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

Security

Your vault is protected by a password you set on first launch. That password is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. We recommend choosing a strong, unique password.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be made available on the App Store listing and within the app. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
